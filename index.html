About YDSA

The future is ours to win

Democratic Socialists of America (DSA) is the largest socialist organization in America, and YDSA is its youth & campus wing. DSA has over 66,000 members, including Congresswomen Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez & Rashida Tlaib. As socialists, we believe that:

  • The economy should be run democratically by workers
  • The liberation of all oppressed peoples requires the abolition of capitalism
  • The US imperial empire must be dismantled globally
  • We can only win these victories through working class struggle!

Central Committee

Northeastern YDSA is lead by a democratically elected Central Committee, which establishes chapter priorities, organizes meetings, manages funds, and ensures that the chapter grows and develops in healthy ways.
